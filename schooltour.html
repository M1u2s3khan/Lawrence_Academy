Discover the world-class infrastructure and learning environment at Lawrence Academy.

Infrastructure

  • Encompassed in 4 acres of land with lush green lawns and spacious playfields in quiet and peaceful surroundings is indeed ideal for academic pursuits.
  • Spacious, airy, well-ventilated & well-lit classrooms.
  • A resource center for teachers has also been set up to help teachers update their skills.
  • Specialty Rooms like dance rooms, music rooms, art rooms & audio-visual rooms.
  • Modern & well-equipped physics, chemistry, biology, mathematics, and computer labs.
  • The school cafeteria serving healthy & balanced meals.
  • Air-conditioned libraries, Sports-field, Conference Room.

Library

Libraries give people the opportunity to explore various new theories, experience new ideas, take one person on a wonderful journey, and increase knowledge. Bibliophile people consider the library as their temple. A school library provides information and ideas that are fundamental to functioning successfully in today's information and knowledge-based society.

Not to remain confines to books, sports, and other curricular activities. Our school has a spacious and well-stocked library. A code of conduct is followed to enable the students to have easy and smooth access to academic resources. The school subscribes to various journals, periodicals, daily newspapers, and magazines to inculcate the habit of reading in students as well as teachers.

Laboratory

Lawrence has world-class laboratories that are equipped with the latest and modern facilities. Every year, portions of old labs are completely renovated and refurbished with the latest equipment, furniture, and fixtures.

Keeping in true with modern time Lawrence Academy ensures that practical knowledge is placed on an equal footing with theoretical knowledge for the all-round development of the students. Students are always motivated to perceive learning as fun-filled, analytical, practical, and exciting. For this purpose, the school has laboratories in numerous disciplines that provide in depth knowledge to its pupils. The students-friendly atmosphere in these laboratories helps to enrich the overall personality of the students through motivation and reinforcement.

Playgrounds

Our school prepares students to face a rapidly changing world with confidence and a sense of responsibility to make the world a better place. We provide an adequate environment, infrastructure to pursue sports activities and provide playing opportunities to a wide range of students regardless of gender or cultural backgrounds. We have Basketball, Hockey, Volleyball, Football, and Cricket court in our school. Except for these courts, we also have other indoor and outdoor sports playgrounds.

Transport

Student's safety is our prime responsibility. A school transport facility is provided to students in certain areas on demand. Bus fees are payable in advance along with tuition fees. For withdrawal from the bus service, three months' notice should be given by the parents to school. Safe transport with speed control and one attendant in each bus is available. The transport facility can be withdrawn in case of rules violation, fighting, and ill-treating other students.
