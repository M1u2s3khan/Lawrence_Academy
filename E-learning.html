E-Learning
E-Learning is a futuristic application to access educational curriculum from outside the traditional classrooms. This latest technology brings courses and programs which are delivered and monitored online. Lawrence Academy's own school mobile application from BP Tech Solutions — Building the future — named “Phone Pathsala” is now available on Google Playstore as Lawrence Academy Application.

This application is an innovation for all our students and staff of the school. In this Covid-19 situation, education has changed dramatically, over 1.2 billion children are out of the classroom. But in the 21st century, everything is possible — technology education of your child can be monitored within a few seconds. With our online school application solution “Phone Pathsala” we are one step ahead in school-related education technology. This app provides virtual platforms to conduct online classes easily. Some of the features of Phone Pathsala school application:
  • Easy navigation for managing various school activities and various information of students and teachers through which School Management can manage all the activities of the school.
  • This app gives parents a quick as well as an elaborated update of school circulars, notices, etc.
  • It’s a platform to inform the students and parents of emergencies such as ‘rainy day off’ etc.
  • This app has an attendance portal to update the children’s attendance.
  • A student has easy access to the Timetable, Exam date sheet, classroom schedule, and the teachers can update homework, assignments, worksheets, and class video lectures to the student.
  • School Gallery, one place to post all the memorable moments of all events held in the school. We give an easy platform to show Photos, Events videos, and Promotional pages for parents and students.
