About Us
Lawrence At Glance

Learning is a treasure that follows its owner everywhere.

Lawrence Academy was established in 2011 in Gulaothi with an aim to serve the educational needs. Encompassed in 4 acres of land with lush green lawns and spacious playfields. Our students graduate and continue further studies at several Top Universities. We have years of experience in providing quality and affordable education and received a number of awards for its contribution to education and social service.

Despite its extremely modest beginnings, the school has carved out a niche for itself in the educational scenario with high academic standards and excellence in various co-curricular activities. The main motive of this is the all-round development of students, the school integrated its activities in all spheres such as – art & craft, gardening, declamation, music, photography, dance club, debate, reading, public speaking, drama, recitation, creative writing, fine arts, etc. The school has already won many trophies and much acclaim. Take any platform, Inter-school, District, State, National level, our children have proved themselves the best and have brought honor in every field. The greenery of the school is remarkable and special care is being taken to preserve it. The school campus is well planned with the beautifully laid out tree, plantation, pathways, and flowerbeds beside the well maintained sprawling lawns all around.

Training and exposing young minds through an array of activities like camps, tours, leadership programmes, cultural meets, workshops, cross country races, cycling expeditions and so on, the school garnered multiple awards and accolades at Inter-school, District, State, National and International levels. Our students proved their mettle at various levels bringing honour and laurels for themselves in a plethora of competitions such as in National Debates (Honourable Speaker, Sh. Som Nath Chatterjee honoured our students), Technology (International Informatics Olympiad – only school in the world whose students represented their country in five successive years), Science & Mathematics (National Exhibitions & Aryabhatta Trophy), Social Science (In MUN National Honour), Quiz (Children recently won the trophies and a trip to Japan), Art (Shankar's National Honour), Music (National scholarship to children up to the age of 21), Sports (National level Table Tennis & Skating, State level Basket Ball, Football, Yoga & Gymnastics) or Band (Leading the district march past on Republic Day celebration successively year after year). Lawrence Academy, Gulaothi has always emerged as the best school.

Mission

Our mission is to make Lawrence Academy a Landmark and Torch Bearer of Quality education and establish a world-class center of learning by delivering an educational experience that engages students intellectually, creatively, physically, spiritually, and socially to transcend all barriers and bring a positive change in society.

  • Development of Rural Students through education.
  • Respond to local societal needs by developing selected 'targeted research projects'.
  • Quality training programs in need-based modern technology.
  • To maintain state-of-the-art infrastructure in laboratories.
  • To promote a culture of self-employment.
  • To inculcate moral, ethical, spiritual values in education at all levels.
  • To give to the world – Lawrence – who can win the hearts and minds of others, put service before self, and retain their humility and values even as they soar high and conquer the world.
Vision

The school's vision is to build institutions that uphold the highest professional standards of excellence in education and innovative instructional practices. And to create a global educational community of conscientious individuals who have developed their intellectual, artistic, and professional talents to the highest degree to bring about positive change in society.

Core Values

Lawrence Academy's goal is to become an institution of excellence, where ethical values, character, wisdom, leadership, service, and academic excellence are the foundation for personal and professional growth.

Long Term Goals
  • All information's concerning students/staff to be made available Online.
  • To make the Campus green and chemical-free.
  • To go for the energy auditing of the whole campus.
  • To provide a happy, caring, supportive, and secure environment.
  • To inculcate values central to the ethos of the school and the community.
  • To give to the world – Lawrence – who can win the hearts and minds of others, put service before self, and retain their humility and values even as they soar high and conquer the world.
